Arijit Singh

Arijit Singh is an Indian singer and music composer. He sings

predominantly in Hindi, Bengali, and Telugu but has also performed

in various other Indian languages. He is the recipient of a National

Award and a record six Filmfare Awards. He is also known as

"King of playback singing".
