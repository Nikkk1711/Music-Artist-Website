Ajay-Atul

Ajay–Atul is an Indian composer duo comprising Ajay Gogavale.

and Atul Gogavale. They won the Best Music Direction award

at the 56th National Film Awards from the Government of India

for contributing music to the Marathi film Jogwa in 2008.
