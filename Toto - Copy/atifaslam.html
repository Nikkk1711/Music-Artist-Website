Atif Aslam

Atif Aslam is a Pakistani pop singer. He was born in Wazirabad and

educated in Lahore and Rawalpindi. He is widely recognized in South Asia

and has given several hit songs such as Aadat, Woh Lamhe, Tere Bin,

Pehli Nazar Mein, Tera Hone Laga Hoon, Tu Jaane Na and Meri Kahani.

He is best known for his powerful vocal belting technique.
