Jubin Nautiyal

Jubin Nautiyal is an Indian playback singer and performer.

Jubin was awarded Upcoming Male Vocalist of the Year at 8th

Mirchi Music Awards, 2016 for his song "Zindagi Kuch Toh Bata"

from Bajrangi Bhaijaan, another one of his achievements is the

Rising Musical Star Award received at Zee Business Awards.
