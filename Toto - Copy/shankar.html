Shankar Mahadevan

Shankar Mahadevan is an Indian singer and music composer.

An accomplished musician in Tamil cinema, he is a part of the

Shankar Ehsaan Loy trio team that provides music to Bollywood films.

He completed his Bachelors degree from Ramrao Adik Institute of

Technology, Navi Mumbai.
