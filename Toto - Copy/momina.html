Momina Mustehsan

Momina Mustehsan is a Pakistani singer-songwriter, musician

and social activist. In 2017, BBC named her one of the 100

most influential women, and the following year, Forbes featured

her among its "30 Under 30" Asia list along with nine other

Pakistani individuals.
